2015年我陆陆续续读了将近三十本各类型的非虚构类书籍，这里我想简单记录一下最近两年来读书的体会。

我把读过的书大致有这样的归类：

1. 计算机软件工程学
2. 产品设计和UX设计
3. 创业公司和企业管理
4. 行为经济学和社会心理学
5. 政治学和社会学
6. 历史题材和人物传记
7. 其他类型

下面依次推荐和讨论一下各个分类都有哪些值得推荐的书籍：

1. 计算机软件工程学

	1. [Code Complete (2nd Edition)](http://www.amazon.com/Code-Complete-Practical-Handbook-Construction/dp/0735619670)
[(中文版：代码大全)](http://book.douban.com/subject/1477390/)

		![alt text](/assets/code-complete.jpg)

		软件工程Software Engineering领域内公认的比较全面的权威指南

	2. [The Pragmatic Programmer: From Journeyman to Master](http://www.amazon.com/The-Pragmatic-Programmer-Journeyman-Master/dp/020161622X) [(中文版：程序员修炼之道)](http://book.douban.com/subject/1152111/)

		![alt text](/assets/pragmatic-programmer.jpg)

		软件工程Software Engineering领域内的最为经典的实用主义指南
	
	3. [Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship](http://www.amazon.com/Clean-Code-Handbook-Software-Craftsmanship/dp/0132350882) [(中文版：代码整洁之道)](http://book.douban.com/subject/4199741/)

		![alt text](/assets/clean-code.jpg)

		软件工程Software Engineering领域内的另一本经典的实用主义指南

2. 产品设计和UX设计

	1. [Don't Make Me Think, Revisited: A Common Sense Approach to Web Usability (3rd Edition)](http://www.amazon.com/Dont-Make-Think-Revisited-Usability/dp/0321965515/)

		![alt text](/assets/don't-make-me-think.jpg)
	
		深入浅出的讲解互联网可用性Usability设计的经典读物

	2. [The Design of Everyday Things: Revised and Expanded Edition](http://www.amazon.com/The-Design-Everyday-Things-Expanded/dp/0465050654)

		![alt text](/assets/the-design-of-everyday-things.jpg)

		现实生活中的可用性Usability设计的经典读物

	3. [Hooked: How to Build Habit-Forming Products](http://www.amazon.com/Hooked-How-Build-Habit-Forming-Products/dp/1591847788/)

		![alt text](/assets/hooked.jpg)

		互联网时代如何设计让用户欲罢不能的产品

	4. [100 Things Every Designer Needs to Know About People](http://www.amazon.com/Things-Designer-People-Voices-Matter/dp/0321767535)

		![alt text](/assets/100-things-every-design-needs-to-know-about-people.jpg)

		100条关于设计和心理学的小常识和小建议，详见本站文章[100 Things about Design and Psychology](http://dhei.github.io/2015/12/13/100-things-about-UX/)

	5. [Evil By Design](http://www.amazon.com/Evil-Design-Interaction-Lead-Temptation/dp/1118422147)

		![alt text](/assets/evil-by-design.jpg)

		互联网时代的产品往往利用了人性的弱点来钩住用户，而这本书的借用宗教当中的七宗罪（Pride, Sloth, Gluttony, Anger, Envy, Lust & Greed）来描述人性中与生俱来的弱点。这本书可读性很强，阅读体验不错。

3. 创业公司和企业管理 

	1. [Zero to One: Notes on Startups, or How to Build the Future](http://www.amazon.com/Zero-One-Notes-Startups-Future/dp/0804139296)
[(中文版：从0到1)](http://book.douban.com/subject/24753651/)

		![alt text](/assets/zero-to-one.jpg)

		Peter Thiel的创业哲学，关于如何避免竞争、如何进行垄断、如何发现新的市场的论述非常经典

	2. [The Lean Startup: How Today's Entrepreneurs Use Continuous Innovation to Create Radically Successful Businesses](http://www.amazon.com/Lean-Startup-Entrepreneurs-Continuous-Innovation/dp/0307887898/)
[(中文版：精益创业)](https://book.douban.com/subject/10945606/)

		![alt text](/assets/the-lean-startup.jpg)

		近年来流行的“精益创业”创业理念的开山之作，关于Minimal-Viable-Product和如何Continuous innovation的论述非常精彩

	3. [The Hard Thing About Hard Things: Building a Business When There Are No Easy Answers](http://www.amazon.com/The-Hard-Thing-About-Things/dp/0062273205/)
[(中文版：创业维艰)](http://book.douban.com/subject/26306686/)

		![alt text](/assets/the-hard-thing-about-hard-things.jpg)

		创业型公司在发展过程中的管理技巧与细节——当事情已经搞砸时，你该怎么办

	4. [How Google Works](http://www.amazon.com/How-Google-Works-Eric-Schmidt/dp/1455582344/)

		![alt text](/assets/how-google-works.jpg)

		Google执行主席施密特[Eric Schmidt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eric_Schmidt)给自家公司撰写的的比较有诚意的软文合集，重点介绍Google早年发展过程中（2000年那个时代）独具一格的公司文化，很有信息量，推荐任何想了解Google早年发家史的人去读。当然，今日的巨头Google据说早已不是这样子的了。

5. 政治学和社会学

	1. [Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind](http://www.amazon.com/Sapiens-Humankind-Yuval-Noah-Harari/dp/0062316095/)
[(中文版：人类简史)](https://book.douban.com/subject/25985021/)

		![alt text](/assets/sapiens-a-brief-history-of-humankind.jpg)

		2015年年度最精彩的书籍，横跨多学科探讨人类几百万年社会/政治/经济/文化的演化历史，视角独特，观点极有说服力，能够改变我们看待人类社会自身世界观的一本奇书。

	2. [Pedigree: How Elite Students Get Elite Jobs](http://www.amazon.com/Pedigree-How-Elite-Students-Jobs/dp/0691155623/)

		![alt text](/assets/pedigree.jpg)
	
		Americans are taught to believe that upward mobility is possible for anyone who is willing to work hard, regardless of their social status, yet it is often those from affluent backgrounds who land the best jobs. Pedigree takes readers behind the closed doors of top-tier investment banks, consulting firms, and law firms to reveal the truth about who really gets hired for the nation's highest-paying entry-level jobs, who doesn't, and why.

	3. [America Alone: The End of the World As We Know It](http://www.amazon.com/America-Alone-End-World-Know/dp/1596985275/)

		![alt text](/assets/america-alone.jpg)

		知名的美国保守派学者Mark Steyn关于欧洲穆斯林化与西方的衰落的经典论述，美国科罗拉多大学物理学家万维钢老师[(@GK同人于野)](http://weibo.com/geekonomics)在微博上有一篇对于此书精彩的[读书笔记](http://weibo.com/p/1001603796644201140832)值得推荐

	4. [The Triple Package: How Three Unlikely Traits Explain the Rise and Fall of Cultural Groups in America](http://www.amazon.com/Triple-Package-Unlikely-Explain-Cultural/dp/0143126350/)

		![alt text](/assets/the-triple-package.jpg)

		Americans are taught that everyone is equal, that no group is superior to another. But remarkably, all of America’s most successful groups believe (even if they don’t say so aloud) that they’re exceptional, chosen, superior in some way. Americans are taught that self-esteem—feeling good about yourself—is the key to a successful life. But in all of America’s most successful groups, people tend to feel insecure, inadequate, that they have to prove themselves. America today spreads a message of immediate gratification, living for the moment. But all of America’s most successful groups cultivate heightened discipline and impulse control.


6. 历史题材和人物传记

	1. [An American Life](http://www.amazon.com/American-Life-Ronald-Reagan/dp/1451628390/)

		![alt text](/assets/an-american-life.jpg)

		二战以后美国历史上最受欢迎的总统——里根——自传

	2. [41: A Portrait of My Father](http://www.amazon.com/41-Portrait-George-W-Bush/dp/0553447785/)

		![alt text](/assets/41-a-portrait-of-my-father.jpg)

		小布什给他爹老布什总统写的传记，了解布什政治家族的高可信度的一手资料。这本书读罢了解到两点，一方面是家族老一辈革命家的老布什同志从军从商再到从政的白手起家发迹史，另一方面也自述了“富二代”加“官二代”的小布什同志的青年时代的成长经历。书中穿插着小布什特有的幽默感，最让人印象深刻的段子竟然是——晚年的老布什夫妇和卸任后的比尔克林顿尽释前嫌重归于好，克林顿自嘲自己是the black sheep of Bush Family because Barbara Bush would never give up a chance to reclaim another president for Bush family.

	3. [Decision Points](http://www.amazon.com/Decision-Points-George-W-Bush/dp/0307590615/)

		![alt text](/assets/decision-points.jpg)

		二战以后美国历史上最不受待见的总统——小布什——自传

	4. [Killing Kennedy: The End of Camelot](http://www.amazon.com/Killing-Kennedy-Camelot-Bill-OReilly/dp/0805096663/)

		![alt text](/assets/killing-kennedy.jpg)

		知名保守派FOX电台主持人[Bill O'Reilly](http://www.billoreilly.com/)的Killing五部曲之一，叙事精彩，可读性强，故事的真实性未必可考，权当野史读一下。这个系列的另外几本是：刺杀耶稣，刺杀林肯，刺杀巴顿，刺杀里根，写这么多本，这该有多大的仇呀。

	5. [A Failed Empire: The Soviet Union in the Cold War from Stalin to Gorbachev](http://www.amazon.com/Failed-Empire-Soviet-Gorbachev-History/dp/0807859583/)

		![alt text](/assets/a-failed-empire.jpg)

		知名冷战史学者Vladislav Zubok从前苏联视角解读冷战史

	6. [文明之光(第一册)](https://book.douban.com/subject/25902942/)

		![alt text](/assets/civilizations-and-enlightenments.jpg)

		《浪潮之巅》的作者吴军的写的世界科技史通俗读物第一册，可读性强，非常有信息量

	7. [文明之光(第二册)](https://book.douban.com/subject/25902222/)

		![alt text](/assets/civilizations-and-enlightenments-2.jpg)

		《浪潮之巅》的作者吴军的写的世界科技史通俗读物第二册，可读性强，非常有信息量

7. 其他类型
	
	1. [The End of Overeating: Taking Control of the Insatiable American Appetite](http://www.amazon.com/End-Overeating-Insatiable-American-Appetite/dp/1605294578/)

		![alt text](/assets/the-end-of-overeating.jpg)

		美国食品药品监管局FDA前任局长David A. Kessler关于工业化的美国食品工业对于美国人恶劣的饮食习惯的控诉。
		
	2. [万万没想到——用理工科思维理解世界](https://book.douban.com/subject/25986341/)

		![alt text](/assets/wan-wan-mei-xiang-dao.jpg)

		美国科罗拉多大学物理学家万维钢老师[(@GK同人于野)](http://weibo.com/geekonomics)的撰写的关于“怎么样用理工科思维理解世界”的书，通俗易懂。我的理解是，这本书谈的都是那些对于接受过严格的理工科科研训练的人来说都是常识性的东西，但对于大部分不具有**批判性思维**能力且**非理工科背景**的人来说是非常不错的“思想启蒙”性质的读物。如果感兴趣请详见本站文章[Practice Makes Genius](http://diheiblog.com//2016/01/18/practice-makes-genius/)
